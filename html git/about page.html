About Us

This is a free collaborative project designed to display applied mathematics. It is an ongoing effort managed by high school students at Bishopshalt School with the purpose of creating simulations and explaining the math principles responsible.

Why Simulate Math?

We believe this website may be useful for other high school students and undergraduate math and physics students to observe use cases of math in real time. This, alongside written explanations, assist with not only visualising underlying principles, but also understanding how and why theorems and ideas come to be.
